About GRANVIORA EVENT MANAGEMENT

Every Event. One Vision. GRANVIORA.

GRANVIORA EVENT MANAGEMENT is a premier luxury event planning company headquartered in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in curating unforgettable experiences for both private and corporate occasions — combining creativity, technology, and flawless execution.

With a vision to turn moments into lifelong memories, Granviora handles everything from elegant weddings and lavish receptions to product launches, fashion shows, award ceremonies, and virtual events. Our commitment lies in transforming client dreams into reality with professionalism, style, and precision.

Founded by Naveen Balaji S, Granviora operates with a core philosophy: “We don’t just plan events — we craft experiences.” Our curated team of designers, planners, and technicians collaborates with the finest vendors and partners to deliver top-tier events with impact and elegance.
